Phantom Airborne Brigade

The Phantom Airborne Brigade is a platform and place for active duty, retired, and or veteran paratroopers to continue jumping in the manner they where used to while serving.

Our Story

Airborne Brotherhood Worldwide

Phantom Airborne Brigade Inc. founded in 1996 has focused on supporting the Airborne community, emphasizing the impact and scale of its efforts rather than financial success.

  • Established in the 1980's by Military Trained Airborne Personnel
  • Continues to support Active Duty, Retired, Veteran and Disabled Paratroopers
  • With a total of 25,000 descents without equipment malfunctions
  • Open to ALL Members of the Militery Trained Airborne Brotherhood
